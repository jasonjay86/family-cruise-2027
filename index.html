The Itinerary

Six days. Galveston → Caribbean → Galveston.

Day 1 · Sun Embarkation
Galveston, TX

San Antonio → Galveston by 11:30am. Board at noon. Sail-away party at 3:30pm as Texas fades behind us. Dinner in the main dining room, welcome show in the Grand Central atrium.

Day 2 · Mon Sea Day
Gulf of Mexico

Ride BOLT. Trivia. Pool. Sea Day Brunch. The ship is the destination today.

Day 3 · Tue Cozumel, Mexico
9am–5pm · Caribbean Sea

Snorkel Chankanaab Park ($25/pp) or lounge at Mr. Sancho's all-inclusive beach club ($60/pp). Crystal-clear water, sea lion show, reef snorkeling. Back on board by 4:30 — the ship waits for no one.

Day 4 · Wed Isla Tropicale, Honduras
9am–6pm · Honduras

🌴 Honduras — not Mexico. Carnival's private beach paradise. White sand, crystal water, beach chairs, snorkeling, kayaking, beach BBQ. No taxis, no ruins, no negotiating — just walk off the ship and onto the sand. Long, lazy beach day with a generous 6pm departure.

Day 5 · Thu Sea Day
Caribbean Sea

Sleep in. Bingo. Waterslides. One last BOLT ride. Elegant Night — the second formal evening. Theater show. Soak in every moment before the voyage winds down.

Day 6 · Fri Sea Day + Pack
Gulf of Mexico

Final sea day. Last Guy's Burger. Final round of mini-golf. Pack in the afternoon — luggage out by 10pm. One last dinner together. Debarkation talk.

Day 7 · Sat Homeward
Galveston → San Antonio

Ship docks at 7am. Breakfast on board. Debarkation by 10am. Back in San Antonio by 2pm — with 1,000 photos and a family that just had the trip of a lifetime.
